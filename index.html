GOD'S AWE WORSHIP MINISTRIES

The Ministry Foundations

God’s Awe Worship Ministries is a Bible-based ministry, non-profit Christian organization laid on the foundation of prayer and the Rhema word of God. This ministry is called to share the Gospel of Jesus, disciple nations, and raise a generation who shall worship the Father in spirit and truth.

Gods Awe Worship Center Ministries was begun as a prayer fellowship in January 2020 by Rev. Jeremy and Tabitha Mwenda. By God's grace, on September 27, 2020, it was established as a ministry based in Abilene, Texas, United States of America.

Since its inception, the ministry has truly been Pentecostal, indigenous, self-supporting, and self-governing. The fast-growing ministry has continued to attract and minister to large numbers of people, locally and globally, both young and old in all walks of life. The need to operate as a church with all biblical sacraments and forms of worship was fully realized on the 27th of September 2020.

"The name God’s Awe Worship Center Ministries was prayerfully derived from Hebrews 12:28: 'Therefore, let us be grateful for receiving a kingdom that cannot be shaken, and thus let us offer to God acceptable worship, with reverence and Awe for our God is a consuming fire.' This was preferred and adopted immediately to reflect the true and main ministry and vision of the church."

Statement of Faith

01

The Holy Scriptures

The Bible is the inspired, authoritative, inerrant, and infallible Word of God. (2 Pet 1:21, 2 Tim 3:16)

02

The Holy Trinity

One God eternally existent in three separate persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. These three are one. (2 Cor 13:14)

03

Deity of Jesus Christ

His virgin birth, atoning sacrifice, resurrection, ascension, and literal pre-millennial return to earth to reign for 1,000 years with His saints. (Jn 10:30, Phil 2:6, 1 Thess 4:16-17, Rev 20:4)

04

The Church Body

The universal and local bodies acting as vehicles for corporate worship and structural fulfillment of the Great Commission. (Eph 1:22-23, Heb 10:25)

05

Salvation & Holy Spirit

Personal salvation through grace upon heart confession, coupled with the initial evidence of speaking in tongues and the fruits of the Spirit. (Acts 2:1-4, Rom 10:9-10, Eph 2:8)

06

Water Baptism

Executed strictly via complete water immersion after personal salvation to declare dying to sin and rising with Jesus in newness of life. (Matt 28:19, Rom 6:4)

07

Divine Healing & Medicine

Obtained through the atonement and active faith in our healer, Jesus Christ. We also firmly believe in professional medical care. (Jas 5:14-15, 1 Pet 2:24)

08

Satan & Eternal Judgment

Satan is a restrained entity without divine attributes. Demons will be cast out, and along with unwritten names, consigned to everlasting punishment. (Rev 19:20, Rev 20:10-15)
